         NoID: The Patient Identification System

Consent

By clicking “Next” you Agree to the below terms and conditions of participation in the NoID patient identification system. This system will allow for healthcare organizations already engaged in your care to easily identify you across systems.

NoID Terms and Conditions. You must submit two fingerprints unless physically unable in order to participate in NoID. Your fingerprint scans will never leave your healthcare organization, nor will your fingerprint data be stored centrally with any other personally identifiable information. All fingerprint data stored locally has been fully encrypted and abstracted from your personally identifiable information (name, address, etc).

PURPOSE: The requested use or disclosure of my health information is for the following purposes:

  1. To accurately identify me across participating healthcare systems

EXPIRATION: The Authorization expires five years from the date of enrollment unless a different date is specified.

REVOCATION: I understand that I may cancel this Authorization at any time, but must do so by submitting my request for revocation to any participating NoID healhcare organization or using my online patient portal. My revocation will take effect upon receipt or a different date is specified.

NOTICE OF RIGHT AND OTHER INFORMATION:

  1. I understand I do not have to enroll in NoID. My refusal will not affect my ability to seek treatment, payment or eligibility for benefits.
  2. I understand that I have the right to receive a copy of the Authorization.
